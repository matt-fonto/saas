## 6. Content pathways laying out your content GPS

- Content is the lifeblood of any personal brand
- Personal brand -> engine | content -> fuel
- Clear lanes for ideas, videos, posts, articles
- Create a system that brings visibility without taking over your life
- What can I do consistently without burning out?

### Founder Content Idea Machine

1.  Select categories

- Choose 3 categories you want to write about and help people with
- Take your category and be specific on who you are helping with what:
  - by problem
  - by industry
  - by business model

2.  Select approach

- Expert: write about topics you know very much
- Curator: share your learning as an avid learner and curate things from other experts

3. Create 30 content ideas in 30 minutes

- Helpful content ideas
  - Lists: the list of most important skills for a modern full-stack developer
  - Best: the best path to land a job as a developer in Europe
  - Tools: what are the best tools to increase your productivity
  - Facts: 10 facts about the life of a software developer
  - Guides: the guide to organize your life
  - Systems: 5 systems that make you an outstanding software developer
  - Summaries
  - Frameworks
- Deep dive content ideas
  - Data
  - Math
  - Stories
  - Reasons
  - Trend reports
  - Expert quotes
  - Research summary
  - Industry breakdown
- Reflection content ideas
  - Lessons
  - Mistakes
  - Achievements
  - Underrated skills
  - Successful routines
  - Personal experience
  - Advice to yourself 5 years ago
  - 20% traits that make 80% impact
- Vulnerable content ideas
  - Failures
  - Obstacles
  - Insecurities
  - Skills you want to learn
  - What things shaped you
  - What do you value most
  - Things you wish you knew
  - Bad advice your were given
